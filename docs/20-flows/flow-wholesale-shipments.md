# Flow – Wholesale + shipment (Mayoreo / Envíos)

## Story
Orders are larger and can change after the note is created (damage, substitutions).

## Steps
1. Customer requests a list or places an order.
2. Operator creates Sales Note or starts as Quotation.
3. During shipment preparation, quantities or items may change.
4. Operator edits the Sales Note accordingly.
5. Payment may be partial/transfer/credit.

## System requirements
- Sales Note remains editable
- Support partial payments and credit
- Clear audit trail (optional phase 2)
