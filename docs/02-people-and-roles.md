## Roles (real users)
### Owner / Admin (Juan)
- Defines base prices (reference list).
- Oversees suppliers, payments, debts, and high-level reporting.
- Manages user accounts and permissions.

### Operators (Paty, Sheila)
Primary tasks:
- Create Sales Notes (Notas)
- Collections / Accounts Receivable
- Quotations
- Invoices
- Customer communication and follow-up

### Production / Attendant (Nancy)
- Attends the customer at the plant area.
- Helps confirm availability visually.
- May relay/confirm information to operators.

## Permissions (minimal set)
- **Admin**
  - Manage users/permissions
  - Configure folio sequences
  - Edit prices & approve discounts
  - Full reporting
- **Operator**
  - Create/edit sales notes, quotations
  - Manage customers (minimal)
  - Record payments and credits
  - View operational reports
- **Read-only**
  - View notes and reports (for consultation)
